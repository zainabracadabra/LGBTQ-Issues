Although it lacks an impact on others, a person's gender identity and sexuality may increase the rate at which they experience discrimination, violence, and homelessness, among others. A lack of access to this knowledge in turn causes ignorance in society. My mission is to inform about these statistics and providing resources to help communities in need.
