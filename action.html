Take Action

One may donate to organizations such as The Trevor Project to help struggling individuals and/or petition for legislators to ensure the safety and uniform healthcare of LGBTQ+ individuals by codifying the right to equal protections.

Furthermore, there should be no barrier to affirming housing options with supportive services, inclusive of transition-related support, healthcare access, legal services, and mental/emotional support systems for LGBTQ+ youth. Additionally, laws should be passed that prohibit discrimination in access to housing and services for LGBTQ+ youth experiencing homelessness. An increase in funding for housing and services for youth will also alleviate the struggles faced by homeless LGBTQ+ youth.

The creation of care that prioritizes people regardless of their gender or sexuality would greatly benefit LGBTQ+ people who experience sexual, verbal, and physical violence. This would allow victims to undergo proper treatment at police stations, hospitals, shelters or rape crisis centers without their identity impacting the quality of their care.
